Following profiles and viewing your feed

  1. Click the People Button
  2. Click the profile you want to follow
  3. Click the Follow Button.
  4. You can Unfollow by navigating to a followed profile and clicking the Unfollow Button.
  5. Click the Feed Button to view recent posts from those you follow.
